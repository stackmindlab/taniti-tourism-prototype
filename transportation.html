Getting Around Taniti

Visitors have plenty of ways to travel in and around Taniti, whether by air, sea, or land.

Air & Sea Travel

Taniti’s international airport accommodates small jets and prop planes; a larger airport is under construction. Cruise ships dock once a week at Yellow Leaf Bay.

Travel info

Rental Cars & Taxis

Cars are available through a local rental agency, and taxis operate throughout Taniti City for quick and easy trips.

Rental details

Bikes & Walkability

Bikes and mopeds are available for rent, and helmets are a must by law. Buses serve Taniti City and nearby villages. The city is flat, safe, and very walkable
