Island Amenities

From everyday essentials to health services and financial convenience, Taniti has you covered.

Shopping

Taniti has two supermarkets for everyday items, a few smaller grocery stores, and a 24-hour convenience store for late-night needs.

Healthcare

The island only has one hospital and several clinics. Many staff members speak English and other languages.

Banking & Currency

Most businesses accept U.S. dollars and major credit cards. ATMs and banks also provide exchange for euros and yen.
