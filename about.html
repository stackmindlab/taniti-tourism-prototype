About Taniti

Get to know the island’s unique geography, people, and economy.

Geography

Taniti covers less than 500 square miles, with sandy and rocky beaches, a safe harbor, lush rainforests, and a small active volcano in its mountainous interior.

Geography details

People

About 20,000 people live on Taniti. Only younger residents speak English fluently, while older generations in rural areas may speak little or none.

Community info

Economy

Fishing and agriculture once dominated Taniti’s economy, but tourism now plays the central role, while traditional industries remain essential.
