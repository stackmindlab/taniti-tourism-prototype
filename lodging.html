Lodging & Hotels

The island has a four-star beachfront resort, several smaller hotels, family-run guesthouses, and bed-and-breakfasts.

Luxury Resorts

Taniti’s four-star beachfront resort offers ocean-view suites, spas, pools, and fine dining.
