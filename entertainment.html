Things To Do in Taniti

From outdoor adventures to cultural experiences, Taniti entertains everyone, including family-friendly activities like the museum, arcade, bowling, and the new golf course.

Outdoor Adventures

Taniti offers snorkeling in calm coves, rainforest hikes, volcano excursions, deep-sea fishing, and helicopter tours.

Culture & History

Visitors enjoy the island’s history museum, art galleries, and cultural music and dance shows.

Family Fun & Nightlife

Relax in pubs or the microbrewery, dance at the club, see a movie, or enjoy family activities such as bowling, the arcade, the museum, and the new 18-hole golf course.
