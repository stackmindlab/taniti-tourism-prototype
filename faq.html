Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions for first-time visitors to Taniti.

What’s the legal drinking age?

The legal drinking age in Taniti is 18. Alcohol sales are between midnight and 9 a.m.

More info

What type of currency?

Most businesses accept U.S. dollars. Major credit cards are accepted, and banks exchange currency for euros and yen.

Currency details

Is Taniti safe?

Yes. Crime is rare, and visitors should watch for pickpockets only near the busy tourist areas.

Safety tips

Do I need a power adapter?

No. Taniti uses power outlets with 120 volts, the same as those in the United States.

Electrical info

Do locals speak English?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, while older residents in rural areas may not.

Language guide

What healthcare is available?

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics, with multilingual staff to assist visitors.

Health info
